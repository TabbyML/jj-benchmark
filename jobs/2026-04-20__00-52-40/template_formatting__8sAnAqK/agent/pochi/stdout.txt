The alias should format a commit as: `<short_commit_id> | <author_email_local_part> | <first_line_of_description>\n` (e.g., `12345678 | test | Initial commit\n`). *Hint: Use `commit_id.short()`, `author.email().local()`, and `description.first_line()` functions.* 5.
