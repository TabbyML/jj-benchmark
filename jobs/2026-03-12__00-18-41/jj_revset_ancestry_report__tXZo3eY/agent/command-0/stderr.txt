### Script design

The script uses four `jj` revset expressions, one per required analysis:

| Section | Revset | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `::feature-a & ~(::main)` | All ancestors of `feature-a` (tip inclusive) minus all ancestors of `main` — commits exclusive to the feature-a lineage |
| 2 | `::feature-a & ::feature-b & ::feature-c` | Intersection of the three full ancestor sets — the shared history beneath every feature branch |
| 3 | `latest(::feature-a & ::feature-b)` | The single newest commit in the common ancestor set of `feature-a` and `feature-b` — equivalent to `git merge-base` |
| 4 | `files("src/core.py") & (::feature-a \| ::feature-b \| ::feature-c)` | Commits that touched `src/core.py`, restricted to the history reachable from any feature branch |
