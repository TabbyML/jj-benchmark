Identify the **most recent operation** (shown at the top) — it will describe a `squash` action. The operation **immediately below it** (its parent) represents the state of the repo **before** the squash happened. ### Step 2: Inspect the Pre-Squash State Before restoring, verify you have found the correct operation by previewing what the repository looked like at that earlier point.
